Our Story
An interactive tale of learning

Getting Started

1. We started building RESTful APIs several years ago in an effort to unlock our enterprise data. This effort also required that we deliver a Single Sign-ON (SSO) strategy with open standards including SAML and OAuth. We quickly gained traction across our development teams building modern web applications and mashups using these new APIs.


2. Based on the demand of our new web-based micro apps, we discovered an interesting trend. Most of our information workers require read-only access and visibility to small subsystems of data. Coincidentally, we started experimenting with the first generation of iPads and were impressed by there full-day battery life. These two discoveries combined sparked the foundation of our POMA mobile development team in San Francisco.

Looking forward

3. With over two dozen mobile applications under our belts and several major success stories in the field, we are now charging forward focusing on real-time metrics, streaming, and communications solutions both within apps and real-world devices such as sensors.


4. We are weaving our digital touchpoints through social fabric internally and externally. Imagine being able to collaborate with your customers, partners, engineers, and product managers in real-time anywhere and anytime. What if you could integrate real-world products into the chat via sensors and APIs?


5. In tandem, we are embarking on an information strategy which will provide even more intelligence from our yotta-bytes worth of data globally. This strategy will position us to take advantage of Siri-like voice applications and artificial intelligence / predictive modeling and alerting solutions.
